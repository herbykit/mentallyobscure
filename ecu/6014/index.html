Mental Illness: The Research on Developmental Trauma - a guide

Purpose
This guide is intended to assist with learning about how developmental trauma can effect people from a young age until much older, through the ability to identify resources that discuss this form of trauma.

Who needs this?
As a user of Davidson Davie Community College materials, this guide can help you with learning about the research available about developmental trauma, especially for those that expect work in medical or social professions.
Due to the nature of this type of trauma, it effects a person in many contexts, including educationally, socially, emotionally, and relationally.
Example topics that would be helped with this form of research include:

Mental Health
Physical Health
Social skills
