SEARCH METHODS

"Developmental trauma refers to exposure to multiple, cumulative traumatic events, usually of an interpersonal nature, during childhood which results in developmentally adverse consequences." (van der Kolk, 2005, Busuttil, 2009, as cited in Gregorowsk & Seedat, 2013)

Developmentally adverse consequences can include but is not limited to delays in physical, social, behavioural, and cognitive development.
